Why a paper contract works

Every parental-control study and every family therapist lands on the same point: controls imposed silently get fought; rules agreed openly get followed (mostly). A signed agreement gives you a neutral thing to point at — "that's what we all signed" — instead of a nightly negotiation. And the act of filling it in together is the single best conversation about technology your family will have.
