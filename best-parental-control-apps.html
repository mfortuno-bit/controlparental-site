First: do you even need a paid app?

If your kids are under 10, use one platform (all-Apple or all-Microsoft/Android), and your main concern is time limits and adult content — the free built-in tools are genuinely enough. Start with our free guides for iPhone, Windows 11, and YouTube.

A paid app earns its subscription when any of these are true:

  • Your household mixes platforms (iPhone kid + Windows PC + Android tablet) and you want one dashboard.
  • Your kid is on social media or group chats and you want alerts about bullying, predators, or self-harm signals — built-ins don't read content at all.
  • Your kid has learned to bypass the built-in controls.

Our verdict

Kid under 10, single platform: skip paid apps — use the free built-ins.
Tween/teen with social media: Bark — content alerts are the thing built-ins can't do at any price.
Mixed devices, younger kids, want hard limits: Qustodio.
Want one subscription covering kids' safety + family identity/security: Aura.
